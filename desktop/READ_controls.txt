LMB = spawn teal team ship

RMB = spawn purple team ship

C = relinquish control of current ship

Middle mouse = take control of new ship (when no ships are controlled)

Space = fire

Esc = pause

Q/E = zoom in/out

A, D = left and right turn

W, S = Accelerate/decelerate

Point and click to spawn enemies and friendlies in Sandbox mode.
Shoot for points against waves of enemies in Arcade mode. (currently unimplemented)
